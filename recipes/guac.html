Guacamole Recipe

Description

Want to make some delicious, simple, guacamole at home? Try this recipe and follow along. Guacamole is a classic dip that combines well with many dishes. It can even be used as spread with toast. Make guacamole, serve it on the table, and see what you can combine it with!

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe Avocados
  • 1 lime
  • 1/4 cup diced red onion
  • 1 small tomato, diced
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Cut the avocados in half, remove the pits, and scoop the flesh into a bowl.
  2. Squeeze the juice of the lime over the avocado to prevent browning and add a tangy flavor.
  3. Mash the avocados with a fork or a potato masher until you achieve your desired consistency (smooth or slightly chunky).
  4. Add the diced red onion, tomato, cilantro, and minced garlic to the bowl.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Start with a small amount and adjust as needed.
  6. Gently mix all the ingredients together until well combined.
  7. Taste the guacamole and adjust the lime juice, salt, or other ingredients to your preference.
  8. Transfer the guacamole to a serving bowl and cover it with plastic wrap, ensuring the wrap touches the surface of the guacamole to prevent browning.
  9. Refrigerate for about 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
  10. Serve with tortilla chips, vegetable sticks, or as a topping for tacos, burritos, or salads. Enjoy!
